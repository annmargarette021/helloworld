Our Services

What We Provide

  • Root Canal Treatment

    A dental procedure to remove inflamed or infected pulp on the inside of the tooth which is then carefully cleaned and disinfected, then filled and sealed.

  • Dental Extraction

    A tooth extraction may be necessary for many reasons, including severe damage or decay. It can eliminate bacteria and improve your overall oral health.

  • Teeth Cleaning

    Dental cleanings are essential for maintaining optimal oral health and function.

  • Fillings

    Are used to repair worn, decayed or damaged teeth. are a safe and effective way to repair the damage, restore teeth back to their normal shape and function, and prevent further decay.

    • Wisdom Tooth Removal

    Used to remove a tooth from the third set of molars, commonly called your wisdom teeth. The surgery is typically used when the tooth becomes impacted.

  • Orthodontics

    Is a dental specialty focused on aligning your bite and straightening your teeth. Focuses on the diagnosis, prevention, and treatment of conditions affecting the alignment of the teeth and jaw.

  • Dentures

    Rediscover the pleasure of enjoying your favorite foods without worry, speaking with confidence, and smiling without hesitation.

    • Tooth Whitening

    Is a procedure to lighten the color of your teeth and remove stains. It is often an efficient, safe and effective choice for brightening your smile.

    Porcelain Jacket Crowns

    Used to protect the entire surface of a tooth. Crowns restore the function of your teeth, which have been weakened by decay or large fillings. Jacket crowns make your weak tooth stronger and gives it the shape and contour of a natural tooth.
